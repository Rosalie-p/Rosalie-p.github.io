About Me

My name is Rodah Mwikali. I am a passionate and hardworking student currently pursuing a degree in Software Engineering.

I am currently taking courses such as Web Technologies, Data Structures, Algorithms, Object Oriented Programming and Software Design.

After completing my course I hope to work as a professional software developer and build applications that will help people in Kenya and Africa.

I am a friendly, focused and determined person. I enjoy working with others and I am always ready to learn and improve.
